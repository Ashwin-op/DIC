About Us

Design Innovation Centre Spoke at IIITDM Kancheepuram was established in the year 2016 to inculcate, facilitate and spread the culture of innovation among the students / faculty / aspirants and relevant stake holders through Innovative Engineering / Industrial Design oriented novel courses, conduct of special Training / Workshops / Internships on Product Design, Seminars by design experts, organize Design Competitions, Industrial Visits and Outreach Activities.

Learn more

Our Objectives

Education

To develop, enrich and integrate IT enabled design and manufacturing oriented curriculum with technical education.

Inspiration

To inculcate and percolate the culture of innovation for solving the local / indigenous problems.

Innovation

To create and enrich necessary industrial ecosystem for supporting the local industry / society in designing and developing futuristic innovative products.

Our Vision

The Vision of the proposed DIC at IIITDM Kancheepuram is to become a premier hub of excellence in IT enabled Design and Manufacturing education that creates and develops a new generation of engineers and technologists with the ability and mind-set to lead Indian industries in globally competitive economic environment and also to disseminate the knowledge to percolate at the grassroots level.
